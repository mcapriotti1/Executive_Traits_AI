Charles H. Ehlers

BOSTON (AP) — Charles H.
Ehlers, a senior executive of W.R.
Grace & Co., was killed along with
wife, Arlene, in a plane crash Fri-
day on Martha’s Vineyard. He was
62.

Ehlers had been a corporate
vice president at Grace since
1978. In 1992, he was named presi-
dent of the company’s worldwide
container product line, which is
based in Woburn.
